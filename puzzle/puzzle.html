Revolutionary Brush

We have some old toothbrushes here. We like to say that this is one of the most..well, I would say it’s the most important invention from this room,because of the toothbrush, the mouth-as part of our body- it is the one that changed the most during the last hundred year. In Denmark, in a few generations, it has gone from you got all your teeth taken out when you got 14 years old…and in 2 generations, nobody gets that, you can live and have all your teeth intact. So in a very short time, we have totally changed the way our mouth functions and looks; because some of the object over here are fake teeth of course, you could use them to chew if you didn’t have your own teeth.
