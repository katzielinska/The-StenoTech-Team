The clinic is a reconstruction created at the Steno Museum, but most of the interior originates from Dentist Axel Warrer, who for many years had his clinic at Banegårdsplads in Aarhus.

Shortly before his death, he donated all its collection of old instruments for the Jewish Medical History Museum – the current Steno Museum.
